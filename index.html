Research Interest

My research mainly aims at studying spatialized dynamical systems by the means of global transformations. This new mathematical framework comes from a wide variety of known dynamical systems such as cellular automata and Lindenmayer systems.

There is two opposite research direction in this work. On one hand one needs to show how classical dynamical systems can be viewed as special cases of global transformations1. Conversely, new dynamical systems have to be studyied to understand the expressivity of global transformations2.
